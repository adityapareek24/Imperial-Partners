Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 17, "Partnerships for the Goals," focuses on strengthening the means of implementation and revitalizing global partnerships for sustainable development.

It emphasizes the need for enhanced international cooperation, financing, technology transfer, capacity-building, and trade to achieve all 17 SDGs.

SDG 17 aims to foster collaboration between governments, the private sector, civil society, and other stakeholders to address global challenges.

The goal also highlights the importance of data, monitoring, and accountability to ensure progress and effective partnerships.
